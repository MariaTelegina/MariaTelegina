# Maria Telegina

I am a Japanese linguist working on language, identity, cognition, and linguistic diversity.

I am currently a Departmental Lecturer in Japanese Linguistics at the University of Oxford. My research combines cognitive and sociolinguistic approaches with corpus-based, quantitative, and computational methods.

## Current research

* Linguistic identity and language policy in Japan
* Temporal and spatial expression in contemporary Japanese
* Language diversity and AI-mediated communication
* Exploratory evaluation of multilingual and dialectal language-model behaviour

## Selected projects

* **Language in Japanese Policy** — a reproducible corpus and policy-discourse analysis project, currently in preparation
* **Time and Space in Contemporary Japanese** — research on spatial and temporal conceptualisation
* **Japanese Dialects and Language Models** — exploratory research in development
